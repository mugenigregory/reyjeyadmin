# Admin Guide - ShahabElectronics

## How to Access Admin Panel

1. Click the **Menu** icon (☰) in the top-left corner
2. In the sidebar, click **"Admin Login"**
3. Enter credentials:
   - Username: `admin`
   - Password: `admin123`
4. Click **Login**

## Admin Features

### 1. Manage Products
- Add new products with images, prices, descriptions
- Edit existing product details
- Delete products from the catalog
- Set product badges (Best Seller, New, Premium, etc.)
- Assign products to categories

### 2. Inventory Management
- View current stock levels for all products
- Update stock quantities
- Set low stock alert thresholds
- Monitor total inventory value
- Track out-of-stock items
- View low stock alerts

**Stock Indicators:**
- 🟢 Green: In stock (above threshold)
- 🟠 Orange: Low stock (at or below threshold)
- 🔴 Red: Out of stock (0 items)

### 3. Order Tracking
- View all customer orders
- Update order status:
  - Pending
  - Processing
  - Shipped
  - Delivered
  - Cancelled
- See customer details and delivery addresses
- Track order totals and revenue

### 4. Analytics & Finance
- **Financial Overview:**
  - Total revenue (all-time)
  - Monthly revenue with growth percentage
  - Total orders and monthly orders
  - Active customer count

- **Revenue Trends:**
  - Monthly revenue visualization
  - Revenue comparison by month

- **Key Metrics:**
  - Average order value
  - Conversion rate
  - Page views
  - Cart abandonment rate

- **Top Products:**
  - Best-selling products
  - Sales volume per product
  - Revenue by product

## Managing Stock

### Setting Stock Levels
1. Go to **Inventory** from the sidebar
2. Click the **Edit** button (✏️) next to a product
3. Set:
   - **Stock**: Current quantity available
   - **Alert at**: Threshold for low stock warnings
4. Click **Save** (✓)

### Low Stock Alerts
The system automatically alerts when products reach their low stock threshold:
- Orange badge on product cards
- Alert shown in inventory dashboard
- Notification on product detail page

### Out of Stock Handling
When a product has 0 stock:
- Product card shows "Out of Stock" badge in red
- Image appears grayscale
- Add to cart button is disabled
- Customers see clear "unavailable" message

## Managing Orders

### Order Status Workflow
1. **Pending**: New order received
2. **Processing**: Order being prepared
3. **Shipped**: Order dispatched
4. **Delivered**: Order completed
5. **Cancelled**: Order cancelled

### Updating Order Status
1. Click the **Edit** button (✏️) next to order status
2. Select new status from dropdown
3. Click **Save** (✓)
4. Customer receives status update

## Financial Services

### Loan Inquiries
Customers can request loan information via:

- Redirects to WhatsApp with pre-filled inquiry

### Investment Inquiries
Customers can inquire about investments via:
- Sidebar → "Invest With Us"
- Redirects to WhatsApp with investment details request

## Best Practices

### Inventory Management
- ✅ Update stock after each sale
- ✅ Set realistic low stock thresholds
- ✅ Reorder when items hit low stock
- ✅ Remove discontinued items promptly

### Order Management
- ✅ Update order status within 24 hours
- ✅ Process "Processing" orders quickly
- ✅ Confirm delivery before marking "Delivered"
- ✅ Handle cancellations professionally

### Product Management
- ✅ Use high-quality product images
- ✅ Write clear, accurate descriptions
- ✅ Price competitively
- ✅ Use badges strategically (don't overuse)
- ✅ Assign correct categories

## Logout
To logout from admin:
1. Open the sidebar
2. Scroll to bottom
3. Click **"Logout"** (red button)

## Security
- 🔒 Change default admin password regularly
- 🔒 Don't share admin credentials
- 🔒 Logout after each session
- 🔒 Access admin panel only on secure networks
